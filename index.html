New Talent: Interior Designers to Watch

Ashe + Leandro

With a knack for creating relaxed, yet modren luxury spaces, the two designers are reinventing contemporary living. This spring, the duo is launching a furniture line just in time for their 10th anniversary. Notable clients: Jake Gyllenhaal, Liev Schreiber, Seth Meyers, Rashid Johnson, Naomi Watts.

Dan Fink Studios

Classic style gets a contemporary upgrade as Fink simplifies the design process, while keeping traditional touches. He just finished renovating the dancers’ lounge at the American Ballet Theatre practice studio. Notable clients: Facebook co-founder Chris Hughes, Peter Thiel and Thiel's PayPal co-founder, Max Levchin.

Ryan Korban

Synonymous with luxury, Ryan is most known for working with fashion brands as Balenciaga, Aquazzura, and Altuzarra. He just completed a 12-story, 61-unit luxury residential building in the heart of New York’s NoHo neighborhood. Notable clients: Alexander Wang, James Franco, Kanye West, Natasha Poly, Debra Messing.
